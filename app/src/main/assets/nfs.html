Prohibited Items.

  1. Mace and pepper sprays are not permitted in carry-on or checked baggage.
  2. Fireworks, gunpowder, flares, flare guns and fireworks novelties such as holiday poppers or "Christmas crackers" are not allowed in carry-on or checked baggage.
  3. Firearm arms and ammunition
  4. Gasoline-powered tools and equipment, such as chainsaws
  5. Bleach, drain cleaners, epoxy, fuel, gel fuel, glue, insecticides
  6. Tasers are not allowed in carry-on or checked baggage.
  7. Shock absorbers may contain compressed gas or residual vapors or gasoline, and therefore are not permitted in checked or carry-on baggage.
  8. Explosive Materials-Blasting Caps, Dynamite, Fireworks, Flares (in any form), Hand Grenades, Plastic Explosives, Realistic Replicas of Explosives.
  9. Flammable Items : Gasoline, Gas Torches, Lighter Fluid, Strike anywhere matches, Turpentine and Paint Thinner, Realistic Replicas of Incendiaries
  10. Compressed gases.
  11. Avalanche packs are not permitted in carry-on or checked baggage.
  12. Aerosol (any except for personal care or toiletries in limited quantities)
  13. Fuels (including cooking fuels and any flammable liquid fuel)
  14. Gasoline, Gas Torches, Lighter Fluid, Flammable Paints, Turpentine and Paint Thinner, Realistic Replicas of Incendiaries
  15. Chlorine for Pools and Spas, Liquid Bleach, Tear Gas,
  16. Drug, Marijuana, ALL Illegal drugs
